# Sample Q&A

These are validated sample interactions from the scoped Groww Mutual Fund corpus.

1. Query: What is the expense ratio of Groww Large Cap Fund?
	Answer: The expense ratio of Groww Large Cap Fund is 0.85% for Direct and 2.25% for Regular.
	Citation: https://assets-netstorage.growwmf.in/compliance_docs/Downloads/Expense%20Ratio/Notice%20-%20Change%20in%20TER/2025%20-%202026/06.%20Notice%20-%20Change%20in%20TER_Groww%20Large%20Cap%20Fund.pdf

2. Query: What is the lock-in period for Groww ELSS Tax Saver Fund?
	Answer: The lock-in period for Groww ELSS Tax Saver Fund is 3 years.
	Citation: https://assets-netstorage.growwmf.in/compliance_docs/Downloads/KIM/KIM_Groww%20ELSS%20Tax%20Saver%20Fund.pdf

3. Query: What is the benchmark of Groww Nifty 50 Index Fund?
	Answer: The benchmark of Groww Nifty 50 Index Fund is the Nifty 50 Index - TRI.
	Citation: https://assets-netstorage.growwmf.in/compliance_docs/Downloads/KIM/KIM_Groww%20Nifty%2050%20Index%20Fund.pdf

4. Query: What is the exit load for Groww Large Cap Fund?
	Answer: The exit load is 1% if redeemed or switched out within 7 days from the date of allotment, and Nil if redeemed or switched out after 7 days from the date of allotment.
	Citation: https://assets-netstorage.growwmf.in/compliance_docs/Downloads/SID/SID_Groww%20Large%20Cap%20Fund%20.pdf

5. Query: What is the minimum SIP amount for Groww Banking and Financial Services Fund?
	Answer: The minimum SIP amount is Rs. 100 for daily and weekly SIPs, and Rs. 500 for monthly and quarterly SIPs.
	Citation: https://assets-netstorage.growwmf.in/compliance_docs/Downloads/SID/SID_Groww%20Banking%20and%20Financial%20services%20Fund.pdf

6. Query: How do I download my capital gains statement?
	Answer: Groww Mutual Fund says CAS is not sent for folios where PAN details are not updated, and on a specific request it provides the account statement within 5 business days.
	Citation: https://assets-netstorage.growwmf.in/compliance_docs/Downloads/KIM/KIM_Groww%20Nifty%2050%20Index%20Fund.pdf

7. Query: Should I invest in Groww Large Cap Fund right now?
	Answer: I can only answer factual questions from official mutual fund sources. I cannot suggest whether you should buy, sell, or invest in a scheme.
	Citation: https://investor.sebi.gov.in/
